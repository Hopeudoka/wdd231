Welcome to Lagos!

Lekki Conservation Centre

19 Lekki - Epe Expy, Lekki Penninsula II 106104, Lagos

A 78-hectare nature reserve with a canopy walkway through the treetops, offering views of wildlife and the lagoon.

Nike Art Gallery

2, Elegushi Rd, Ikate 106104, Lagos

A four-story art gallery housing about 8,000 diverse artworks from various Nigerian artists.

Tarkwa Bay Beach

Tarkwa Bay Island, Lagos

A sheltered beach near the Lagos Harbor that's popular with swimmers and water sports enthusiasts.

National Museum Lagos

Onikan Rd, Lagos Island, Lagos

Showcases Nigerian art, archaeological and ethnographic exhibits, including Benin Bronzes.

Freedom Park

1 Hospital Rd, Lagos Island, Lagos

A memorial and leisure park built on the site of a former colonial prison, now hosting cultural events.

Eko Atlantic City

Victoria Island, Lagos

A planned city being constructed on land reclaimed from the Atlantic Ocean, dubbed "Africa's Future Hong Kong".

New Afrika Shrine

Pepple St, Ikeja, Lagos

A cultural center and music venue established by Femi Kuti to honor his father Fela Kuti's legacy.

Kajuru Castle

KM 45, Lekki - Epe Expressway, Lagos

A medieval Germany-style castle resort with towers, dungeons, and a crocodile-infested moat.
